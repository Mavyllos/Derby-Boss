About Derby Boss

Spend less time doing things you hate for a sport you love.

We knows that most roller derby legaues are run as a labor of love, not a business. So why waste time using four, five or more different tools, none of which are tailored for your needs, to get league management tasks done?

More Bouts, Less BS

Features

Contact Info

Keep track of league member contact information.

Scheduling

Add, update, and view upcoming games, practices, and other league related events.

Dues Management

Manage membership dues and pull reports to review expiring or overdue memberships.

Emergency Data

Access to emergency contact names, phones numbers, and skater insureance information.

Eligibility Status

Track eligibilty status for members.

Rosters

See a list of all teams, coaches, members, NSOs, referees, and volunteers.
